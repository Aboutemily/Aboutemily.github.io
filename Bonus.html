Health Issues

Goldens have a life span of 10 to 13 years. Some of the Golden Retriever's minor health issues include hypothyroidism sub-aortic stenosis (SAS), eye disorders, elbow dysplasia, mast cell tumors, and seizures. Osteosarcoma is also occasionally seen in Golden Retrievers. Some of it's major health issues are lymphoma, canine hip dysplasia (CHD), hemangiosarcoma, and skin problems.
