Golden Retrievers

Fun Facts

- Golden Retrievers Love swimming
- They are often used on search and rescue teams because of their keen sense of smell and tracking abilities
- Golden Retrievers make great therapy dogs
- Considered to be the 4th smartest dog breed
- They are the third most popular dog breed in America
- There are three type of Golden Retrievers, which vary in color and size
- They are caring and kind because they are also known to care for other animals.. even cats
- Golden Retrievers are easy to train as well
(15 Golden Retriever Fun Facts)

Health Issues

Golden Retrievers have a life span of 10 to 13 years. Some of the Golden Retriever's minor health issues include hypothyroidism sub-aortic stenosis (SAS), eye disorders, elbow dysplasia, mast cell tumors, and seizures. Osteosarcoma is also occasionally seen in Golden Retrievers. Some of it's major health issues are lymphoma, canine hip dysplasia (CHD), hemangiosarcoma, and skin problems.

Care

In order to prevent knots and hair inside the house, its best to brush a golden retriever's fur at least twice a week. Although they can live outdoors the Retriever is at it's best indoors with its family. However it is still important to make sure that a Golden Retriever gets the exercise that they need.

The Golden side To a Golden Retriever

Overall, this type of breed is an amazing dog. They make great family pets, although they tend to be biters so train them early to not fight. Goldens are easy to train and are very caring animals, just don't leave them alone for too long, they'll get depressed or really sad. However Golden Retrievers may be the perfect pet for a family.
